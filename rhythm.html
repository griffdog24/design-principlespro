Rhythm

“A pattern, whether regular or irregular, also may create a sort of movement we could call rhythm. Think of music, foot tapping, finger snapping, clapping and dancing. In graphic design, rhythmic movement has to do with repeating items strategically kind of like a backbeat.”(pp.59)

Something that creates a structure between the website’s pictures and words. It makes a pattern within the website that makes it more pleasing to the reader and easier to follow.
