Continuity

“Our minds will continue a pattern beyond its ending points. Further, our eyes will follow the direction of a line. On the left, our minds see, a single cross shape instead of four shorter converging lines. On the right, ours eyes follow the direction of the A’s swoop and continue on to the star. Applying this concept can add a sense of direction and movement to your layout. Ego, flow.”(pp.62)

Don’t let the page break awkwardly in any way. Make sure all pictures, topics, and paragraphs are finished together. If something is apart from the rest of its similar section, it looks weird and less professional.
