Contrast

“You can employ contrast between filled and empty space. You can employ contrasting sorts of lines or shapes. You may juxtapose contrasting sizes of objects”(pp.56)

Contrast is the obvious difference between two different things or objects. Something that highlights a distinction between objects. Contrast creates a direct line between the two objects being observed.

The contrast between the red brick and the bright aqua green roof top is very apparent. These two colors are contrasting colors because when walking past the building there are very obvious lines where the sides end and the roof begins. The two colors are very different and that brings the building to your attention.
