Emphasis

“The focal point can be anything really, as long as it remains the most eye-catching piece of visual information. Perhaps the focal point becomes so because it sits in a pool of negative space. Perhaps the curve of a line in the layout literally leads to the focal point.” (pp.53)

The focal point is the most eye-catching part of the web page. It’s what the viewer first sees, when the website opens. Without a focal point, the reader doesn’t have something to follow.

This bus has a giant BT sign on the side of the bus. It is a bright white sign, that screams out at your eyes when you are looking at the bus. This means it it the emphasis or focal point of the bus advertisment.
