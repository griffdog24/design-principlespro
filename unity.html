Unity

“The last principle, unity, may seem a little abstract compared to the other five perspectives. Unity means that all the parts of the design work together, and everything looks like it belongs together.”(pp.60)

In the end the most important thing about the website is cohesiveness. The structure and outline must all work together in order to make a well-rounded website that grabs the viewers’ attention and makes them pay attention. That creates unity as everything looks and seems to fit together.
