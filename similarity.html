Similarity

“Our minds group things with similar properties, such as color or shape. ‘like goes with like.’ In this example, we group squares with squares and circles with circles. In layout, we can use similarity to create order and organization through unity.”(pp.62)

When making a website make sure the structure uses a similar template for the entire site. The headlines and paragraphs should be similar to make the page look cohesive.
